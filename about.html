Lighting

We provide a wide range of lights to cater to your preference.
This includes up-lighting ranging through any color combination,
to several overhead lighting options. Our lighting packages are
flexible and accommodating to fully customize your event.

Experienced Dj's

The Hampton Agency provides a DJ that will fit your event.
We take pride in the versatility of our workers to fit the mold of your occasion. From Greek Life events to formal weddings and everything in between, we strive to adjust in your favor. We also live mix and use turntables when appropriate. We have a deep interest for music of all genres, and a passion for entertaining.

Photo Booths

We provide state of the art photo booths. There are several
photo booth rental options, including custom booths, and green
screen. Our photo booths are very popular. They capture the
memories of your special night/day in high definition, with a
wide variety of backgrounds to choose from. Some other features
the photo booth has to offer include the ability to choose a unique
filter, and with built in Wi-Fi you can text the pictures of your
choice directly to your phone!

Biography

We provide premium entertainment to Kentucky, Ohio and Tennessee. Eli Woodie, Founder of The Hampton Agency, has been in the entertainment business for several years. He has been DJ'ing for five years. His passion for music and entertainment drives The Hampton Agency to give you the event experience you are looking for. We are interactive and include live mixing, adjustable to your needs, and bring an energy that will take your event to the next level. Our up lighting provides a professional, beautiful look to any event, our music is chosen based on your interests and our photo booths create another aspect of fun that captures your memories.


Eli began DJ'ing with his older cousin Ross Hill Founder of PictureBooth, when he was 15. They DJ'd weddings, cast parties, homecoming dances, proms, and many other kinds of events together. By the age of 17 Eli began DJ’ing local charity events, high school homecoming dances, and weddings on his own. Eli's cousin Ross mentored and molded him into a very professional, versatile DJ. Eli has only grown since he's taken off as a DJ. He has added to his versatility and provided his DJ, lighting and photo booth services to many events that have been extremely successful.  In consistency with Eli’s base principles of entertainment and customer service, The Hampton Agency strives to contribute in making your night unforgettable in every way possible.
